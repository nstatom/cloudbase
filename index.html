Data Disclaimer



Hourly cloud data (larger circles) is based on hourly airport METAR readings from the NOAA Aviation Weather API. These observations average the past 30 minutes of ceilometer data with double weighting for the last 10 minutes of passing sky condition observations.





High rate cloud data (smaller circles) is only averaged over five minutes from Iowa State Mesonet API. The resulting ceilometer observations are more prone to overall cloud status mischaracterization in patchy cloud conditions than the METAR data since each observation is the result of a point measurement with less averaging.
